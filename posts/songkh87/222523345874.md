I. INTRODUCTION
For voiced sound production, vocal fold vibration modulates airflow through the glottis and produces sound (the voice source), which propagates through the vocal tract and is selectively amplified or attenuated at different frequencies
This selective modification of the voice source spectrum produces perceptible contrasts, which are used to convey different linguistic sounds and meaning. Although this selective modification is an important component of voice production, this review focuses on the voice source and its control within the larynx.
For effective communication of meaning, the voice source, as a carrier for the selective spectral modification by the vocal tract, contains harmonic energy across a large range of frequencies that spans at least the first few acoustic resonances of the vocal tract. In order to be heard over noise, such harmonic energy also has to be reasonably above the noise level within this frequency range, unless a breathy voice quality is desired.
성대가 진동하면 음성이 발생하고 그것을 원음(voice source)이라고 합니다. 그렇게 발생한 소리의 특정 주파수가 성도를 지나며 증폭이 되고 그것이 우리가 듣는 소리가 됩니다. 그리고 어느 주파수가 증폭이 되었냐에 따라 우리는 다른 음질이나 모음으로 듣게 됩니다.
One of the important goals of voice research is to understand how the vocal system produces voice of different source characteristics and how people associate percepts to these characteristics.
While voice production results from a complex fluid-structure-acoustic interaction process, which again depends on the geometry and material properties of the lungs, larynx, and the vocal tract, the end interest of voice is its acoustics and perception.
Although much advancement has been made in understanding the physics of phonation, some misconceptions still exist in textbooks in otolaryngology and speech pathology.
For example, the Bernoulli effect, which has been shown to play a minor role in phonation, is still considered an important factor in initiating and sustaining phonation in many textbooks and reviews.
Tension and stiffness are often used interchangeably despite that they have different physical meanings.
The role of the thyroarytenoid muscle in regulating medial compression of the membranous vocal folds is often understated.
음성 연구의 목표는 발성 기관에 어떤 변화가 있었을 때 그것이 어떻게 음질에 변화를 줄 것인지 예측 하는 것, 그리고 반대로 음질의 변화가 있었을 때 발성 기관에는 어떠한 변화가 있었을지 예측하는 것입니다.
하지만 많은 발전에도 불구하고 아직 여러 오개념들이 널리 퍼져 있습니다. 대표적으로 베르누이의 원리는 성대 진동에서 담당하는 역할이 적은데도 불구하고 아직도 성대 진동의 핵심 원리인 것 처럼 이야기되고 있습니다.
강성(Stiffness)과 장력(Tension)은 엄밀히 다른 물리학적 개념임에도 불구하고 같은 뜻인것 처럼 혼동되어 사용됩니다.
TA근육은 성대 내전에도 중요하게 작용하나, 이에 대해 언급하고 있는 문헌도 많지 않습니다.
II. VOCAL FOLD PHYSIOLOGY AND BIOMECHANICS
A. Vocal fold anatomy and biomechanics
The human vocal system includes the lungs and the lower airway that function to supply air pressure and airflow (a review of the mechanics of the subglottal system can be found in Hixon, 1987), the vocal folds whose vibration modulates the airflow and produces voice source, and the vocal tract that modifies the voice source and thus creates specific output sounds.
폐가 성대에 공기를 보내 성대가 진동하고 그것이 성도를 지나면서 최종적으로 우리가 듣는 소리가 됩니다.
The vocal folds are located in the larynx and form a constriction to the airway. Each vocal fold is about 11–15 mm long in adult women and 17–21 mm in men, and stretches across the larynx along the anteriorposterior direction, attaching anteriorly to the thyroid cartilage and posteriorly to the anterolateral surface of the arytenoid cartilages.
성대는 후두에 위치합니다. 여자는 11~15mm, 남자는 17~21mm. 앞뒤로 늘어나며 앞으로는 갑상연골, 뒤로는 피열연골에 연결됩니다.
Both the arytenoid and thyroid cartilages sit on top of the cricoid cartilage and interact with it through the cricoarytenoid joint and cricothyroid joint, respectively. The relative movement of these cartilages thus provides a means to adjust the geometry, mechanical properties, and position of the vocal folds, as further discussed below.
피열연골과 갑상연골은 윤상연골 위에 얹어져 있는 형태로 존재하며 이러한 연골들의 움직임이 성대의 geometry, mechanical property 그리고 vocal folds position을 결정합니다.
* geometry: 성대가 발성하기 전에 어떤 형태로 만들어지는지를 말합니다
* mechanical property: 성대가 부드러워지거나 뻣뻣해지는 변화를 말합니다
* vocal folds position: 말 그대로 성대의 위치를 말합니다
The three-dimensional airspace between the two opposing vocal folds is the glottis.
The glottis can be divided into a membranous portion, which includes the anterior portion of the glottis and extends from the anterior commissure to the vocal process of the arytenoid, and a cartilaginous portion, which is the posterior space between the arytenoid cartilages.
성대 사이를 성문(glottis)이라고 하며 앞을 막양부(membranous portion), 뒤를 연골부(cartilagenous portion)이라고 부릅니다. 연골부는 피열연골의 사이에 위치합니다.
* 여기서는 성대 사이를 성문이라고 칭하나 문헌에 따라서는 성문열이라고 하고 성문은 성대와 성문열을 합한 개념으로 설명하기도 하는 듯 합니다.
The vocal folds are layered structures, consisting of an inner muscular layer (the thyroarytenoid muscle) with muscle fibers aligned primarily along the anterior-posterior direction, a soft tissue layer of the lamina propria, and an outmost epithelium layer.
성대는 근육층, 고유층, 상피층으로 이루어집니다.
The thyroarytenoid (TA) muscle is sometimes divided into a medial and a lateral bundle, with each bundle responsible for a certain vocal fold posturing function. However, such functional division is still a topic of debate (Zemlin, 1997).
TA는 안쪽(medial)과 바깥쪽(lateral)의 두 다발로 이루어져 있는데 기능이 명확히 구분되는지는 아직 확실히 밝혀지지는 않았습니다.
The lamina propria consists of the extracellular matrix (ECM) and interstitial substances. The two primary ECM proteins are the collagen and elastin fibers, which are aligned mostly along the length of the vocal folds in the anterior-posterior direction (Gray et al., 2000).
고유층은 콜라겐과 엘라스틴 섬유로 이루어져있습니다.
* 어떤 컨퍼런스에서는 성대의 성상을 푸딩, 그리고 구미베어로 설명했었는데 이게 상당히 직관적이었습니다.
Based on the density of the collagen and elastin fibers , the lamina propria can be divided into a superficial layer with limited and loose elastin and collagen fibers, an intermediate layer of dominantly elastin fibers, and a deep layer of mostly dense collagen fibers (Hirano and Kakita, 1985; Kutty and Webb, 2009). In comparison, the lamina propria (about 1 mm thick) is much thinner than the TA muscle.
고유층은 바깥쪽일수록 성기고 깊어질수록 조밀해집니다.
Conceptually, the vocal fold is often simplified into a two-layer body-cover structure (Hirano, 1974; Hirano and Kakita, 1985).
이를 단순화하여 바디-커버 이론으로 설명하기도 합니다.
The body layer includes the muscular layer and the deep layer of the lamina propria, and the cover layer includes the intermediate and superficial lamina propria and the epithelium layer.
Hirano는 근육과 고유층 심층(deep layer)를 바디, 그리고 상피, 고유층 천층(superficial layer), 중간층(intermediate layer)을 커버로 분류했습니다.
In addition to a body and a cover layer, the intermediate and deep layers of the lamina propria are grouped into a vocal ligament layer (Hirano, 1975). It is hypothesized that this layered structure plays a functional role in phonation, with different combinations of mechanical properties in different layers leading to production of different voice source characteristics (Hirano, 1974).
고유층의 심층과 중간층을 묶어서 성대 인대(vocal ligament)층으로 명명하기도 했습니다. 이 구조의 구성 차이가 사람들간의 목소리 음질 차이를 만드는데 핵심적인 역할을 하는 듯 합니다.
However, because of lack of data of the mechanical properties in each vocal fold layer and how they vary at different conditions of laryngeal muscle activation, a definite understanding of the functional roles of each vocal fold layer is still missing.
하지만 근육 활성도에 따라 이러한 성상이 다양하게 변하기 때문에 각 층의 정확한 역할은 아직 밝혀지지 않았습니다.
These studies showed that the vocal folds exhibit a nonlinear, anisotropic, viscoelastic behavior.
성대의 중요한 3가지 물리학적 특징은 비선형(nonlinear), 비등방성(anisotropic), 점탄성(viscoelastic)입니다.
* 비선형(nonlinear): 힘을 가했을때 일정하게 성질이 변하는게 아니라 가할수록 성질이 변하는 정도가 달라지는것을 말하는 듯 합니다. 고무줄을 늘릴 때 늘어난 상태에서는 같은 길이를 늘리기 위해 힘을 더 주어야 하는 걸 생각하시면 될 것 같습니다.
* 비등방성(anisotropic): 방향에 따라 물체의 성질이 달라지는 것을 뜻합니다. 예를 들어 어떤 이파리의 경우 세로로는 잘 찢어지고 가로로는 잘 찢어지지 않는데 그런 특성을 생각하시면 될 것 같습니다.
* 점탄성(viscoelastic): 점성과 탄성이 함께 있다는 말로 위에서 설명한 푸딩이나 구미베어의 느낌을 생각하시면 될 것 같습니다.
A typical stress-strain curve of the vocal folds under anterior-posterior tensile test is shown in Fig. 2. The slope of the curve, or stiffness, quantifies the extent to which the vocal folds resist deformation in response to an applied force. In general, after an initial linear range, the slope of the stress-strain curve (stiffness) increases gradually with further increase in the strain, presumably due to the gradual engagement of the collagen fibers.
Such nonlinear mechanical behavior provides a means to regulate vocal fold stiffness and tension through vocal fold elongation or shortening, which plays an important role in the control of the F0 or pitch of voice production.

쉽게 말해 성대가 늘어날수록 더 늘리기 빡세집니다.
Typically, the stress is higher during loading than unloading, indicating a viscous behavior of the vocal folds.
그리고 성대를 늘리는게 푸는 것보다 빡셉니다.
Due to the presence of the AP-aligned collagen, elastin, and muscle fibers, the vocal folds also exhibit anisotropic mechanical properties, stiffer along the AP direction than in the transverse plane. Experiments (Hirano and Kakita, 1985; Alipour and Vigmostad, 2012; Miri et al., 2012; Kelleher et al., 2013a) showed that the Young’s modulus along the AP direction in the cover layer is more than 10 times (as high as 80 times in Kelleher et al., 2013a) larger than in the transverse plane.
콜라겐, 엘라스틴 섬유들이 앞뒤로 배열되어 있어서 성대는 앞뒤로 늘리는게 다른 방향보다 훨씬 빡셉니다.
Stiffness anisotropy has been shown to facilitate medial-lateral motion of the vocal folds (Zhang, 2014) and complete glottal closure during phonation (Xuan and Zhang, 2014).
이러한 성대의 특성이 성대가 쉽게 닫힐 수 있게(facilitate medial-lateral motion, complete glottal closure) 해줍니다.
Accurate measurement of vocal fold mechanical properties at typical phonation conditions is challenging, due to both the small size of the vocal folds and the relatively high frequency of phonation.
성대는 너무 빠르게 떨리고 작아서 여러 mechanical property를 측정하기 어렵습니다.
Although tensile tests and shear rheometry allow direct measurement of material modules, the small sample size often leads to difficulties in mounting tissue samples to the testing equipment, thus creating concerns of accuracy.
Tensile test나 shear rheometry같은 방법들로 성대의 물리학적 특성을 연구하려는 시도는 하는데 성대가 너무 작아서 오류가 나기 쉽습니다. 그래서 결과를 곧이곧대로 믿기 좀 그렇습니다.
These two methods also require dissecting tissue samples from the vocal folds and the laryngeal framework, making it impossible for in vivo measurement.
그리고 측정하려면 조직을 떼어야 하는데 그래서 살아있는 사람에게 연구할 수도 없습니다.
The indentation method is ideal for in vivo measurement and, because of the small size of indenters used, allows characterization of the spatial variation of mechanical properties of the vocal folds.
Indentation methods 는 그나마 쓸만합니다.
However, it is limited for measurement of mechanical properties at conditions of small deformation. Although large indentation depths can be used, data interpretation becomes difficult and thus it is not suitable for assessment of the nonlinear mechanical properties of the vocal folds.
그래도 어렵습니다.
There has been some recent work toward understanding the contribution of individual ECM components to the macro-mechanical properties of the vocal folds and developing a structurally based constitutive model of the vocal folds (e.g., Chan et al., 2001; Kelleher et al., 2013b; Miri et al., 2013).
최근에는 성대의 구성방정식(constitutive equation)을 구하려는 주목할만한 성과가 있었습니다.
* 구성방정식: 변형 가능한 물체에 외부 힘이 작용하였을 때 발생한 내부 저항력과 변형도 간의 관계식
The contribution of interstitial fluid to the viscoelastic properties of the vocal folds and vocal fold stress during vocal fold vibration and collision has also been investigated using a biphasic model of the vocal folds in which the vocal fold was modeled as a solid phase interacting with an interstitial fluid phase (Zhang et al., 2008; Tao et al., 2009, Tao et al., 2010; Bhattacharya and Siegmund, 2013)
This structurally based approach has the potential to predict vocal fold mechanical properties from the distribution of collagen and elastin fibers and interstitial fluids, which may provide new insights toward the differential mechanical properties between different vocal fold layers at different physiologic conditions.
콜라겐과 엘라스틴 그리고 간질액 등을 고려하여 여러가지 상황에서 성대의 mechanical property 변화를 예측하려는 여러가지 연구가 현재에도 시도되고 있습니다.
