III. PHYSICS OF VOICE PRODUCTION
A. Sound sources of voice production
The phonation process starts from the adduction of the vocal folds, which approximates the vocal folds to reduce or close the glottis.
발성의 시작은 성대의 내전입니다. 성대의 양쪽 부분이 서로 접근하여 성문을 닫아주는 것을 뜻합니다.
*성문(glottis): 성대 사이의 공간
Contraction of the lungs initiates airflow and establishes pressure buildup below the glottis. When the subglottal pressure exceeds a certain threshold pressure, the vocal folds are excited into a self-sustained vibration.
폐가 수축하면 닫힌 성대 아래로 압력이 형성되고 특정 압력, 즉 역치 압력(threshold pressure)을 넘어가게 되면 성대는 자가 진동 모드(self-sustained vibration)로 전환되게 됩니다.
Vocal fold vibration in turn modulates the glottal airflow into a pulsating jet flow, which eventually develops into turbulent flow into the vocal tract.
성대가 열고 닫힘에 따라 그 사이로 제트 기류가 발생하고 결과적으로 성도에서 난류를 발생시킵니다.
In general, three major sound production mechanisms are involved in this process (McGowan, 1988; Hofmans, 1998; Zhao et al., 2002; Zhang et al., 2002a), including a monopole sound source due to volume of air displaced by vocal fold vibration, a dipole sound source due to the fluctuating force applied by the vocal folds to the airflow, and a quadrupole sound source due to turbulence developed immediately downstream of the glottal exit.
소리는 단극자(monopole), 쌍극자(dipole), 난류음(quadrupole)의 세 메커니즘으로 발생합니다.
When the false vocal folds are tightly adducted, an additional dipole source may arise as the glottal jet impinges onto the false vocal folds (Zhang et al., 2002b).
가성대가 강하게 내전된 상태에서는 추가적인 쌍극자(dipole)음이 발생하기도 합니다.
The monopole sound source is generally small considering that the vocal folds are nearly incompressible and thus the net volume flow displacement is small.
단극자(monopole)음이 차지하는 비중은 적습니다.
The dipole source is generally considered as the dominant sound source and is responsible for the harmonic component of the produced sound.
우리가 주로 이야기하는 성대 원음은 쌍극자(dipole)음입니다.
The quadrupole sound source is generally much weaker than the dipole source in magnitude, but it is responsible for broadband sound production at high frequencies.
난류음(quadrupole) 역시 그 크기가 매우 약하지만 고음역대에서 어느정도 역할이 있습니다.
For the harmonic component of the voice source, an equivalent monopole sound source can be defined at a plane just downstream of the region of major sound sources, with the source strength equal to the instantaneous pulsating glottal volume flow rate. In the source-filter theory of phonation (Fant, 1970), this monopole sound source is the input signal to the vocal tract, which acts as a filter and shapes the sound source spectrum into different sounds before they are radiated from the mouth to the open as the voice we hear.
발성을 설명하는 소스 필터 이론에서는 이 단극자음(monopole)이 필터로 작용하여 성대 원음을 디자인하는 것으로 설명합니다.
Because of radiation from the mouth, the sound source is proportional to the time derivative of the glottal flow. Thus, in the voice literature, the time derivate of the glottal flow, instead of the glottal flow, is considered as the voice source.
성대 원음은 기류가 아닌 기류의 시간에 대한 미분값과 비례합니다.
* 기류가 U라면 dU/dt에 비례한다는 뜻입니다.
The phonation cycle is often divided into an open phase, in which the glottis opens (the opening phase) and closes (the closing phase), and a closed phase, in which the glottis is closed or remains a minimum opening area when the glottal closure is incomplete.
성대 진동은 성대가 열려 있는 개대기(open phase)와 닫혀있는 폐쇄기(closed phase)로 나눕니다.
The glottal flow increases and decreases in the open phase, and remains zero during the closed phase or minimum for incomplete glottal closure (Fig. (Fig.4).4).
개대기에는 기류가 증가하거나 감소하고 폐쇄기에는 기류가 거의 없거나 존재하지 않습니다.
Compared to the glottal area waveform, the glottal flow waveform reaches its peak at a later time in the cycle so that the glottal flow waveform is more skewed to the right.
성문 기류의 변화는 성문 면적의 변화를 한 템포 늦게 따라갑니다.
This skewing in the glottal flow waveform to the right is due to the acoustic mass in the glottis and the vocal tract (when the F0 is lower than a nearby vocal tract resonance frequency), which causes a delay in the increase in the glottal flow during the opening phase, and a faster decay in the glottal flow during the closing phase (Rothenberg, 1981; Fant, 1982).
이러한 특성 때문에 성문이 열릴 땐 기류의 증가가 약간 더디고 닫힐 땐 기류의 증가가 더 빠릅니다.
Because of this waveform skewing to the right, the negative peak of the time derivative of the glottal flow in the closing phase is often much more dominant than the positive peak in the opening phase.
그래서 기류의 미분값, 그러니까 기류의 기울기는 열릴때보다 닫힐때 가파릅니다.
The instant of the most negative peak is thus considered the point of main excitation of the vocal tract and the corresponding negative peak, also referred to as the maximum flow declination rate (MFDR), is a major determinant of the peak amplitude of the produced voice.
그래서 이 성문이 닫힐 때 기류의 기울기를 MFDR(maximum flow declination rate)라고 부르는데 이 값이 목소리의 크기를 결정하는 가장 핵심적인 값입니다.
After the negative peak, the time derivative of the glottal flow waveform returns to zero as phonation enters the closed phase.
성대가 폐쇄기에 도달하면 기류의 미분값은 0이 됩니다.

![image](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC5412481/bin/JASMAN-000140-002614_1-g004.jpg)

Much work has been done to directly link features of the glottal flow waveform to voice acoustics and potentially voice quality (e.g., Fant, 1979, 1982; Fant et al., 1985; Gobl and Chasaide, 2010).
기류 그래프와 목소리의 음향, 그리고 음질과의 관계를 많은 사람들이 연구했습니다.
These studies showed that the low-frequency spectral shape (the first few harmonics) of the voice source is primarily determined by the relative duration of the open phase with respect to the oscillation period (To/T in Fig. Fig.4,4, also referred to as the open quotient).
저주파수 배음들의 스펙트럼 모양은 주로 성문 개대율(open quotient)에 의해 결정된다는 것을 발견했습니다.
A longer open phase often leads to a more dominant first harmonic (H1) in the low-frequency portion of the resulting voice source spectrum.
성대가 오래 열려 있을수록, 즉 성대 개대율이 높을수록 첫번째 배음(H1)이 두드러졌습니다.
For a given oscillation period, shortening the open phrase causes most of the glottal flow change to occur within a duration (To) that is increasingly shorter than the period T. This leads to an energy boost in the low-frequency portion of the source spectrum that peaks around a frequency of 1/To.
성대 개대율이 감소하면 1/To에 해당하는 주파수의 스펙트럼이 증폭되었습니다.
For a glottal flow waveform of a very short open phase, the second harmonic (H2) or even the fourth harmonic (H4) may become the most dominant harmonic.
성대 개대율이 매우 작으면 증폭되는 주파수가 증가하기 때문에 H2, 심지어는 H4가 증폭되어 주 배음(dominant harmonics)이 되기도 했습니다.
Voice source with a weak H1 relative to H2 or H4 is often associated with a pressed voice quality.
이렇게 H1이 H2 혹은 H4보다 약해지면 소리는 과접촉된 소리로 들립니다.
The spectral slope in the high-frequency range is primarily related to the degree of discontinuity in the time derivative of the glottal flow waveform.
스펙트럼의 기울기(spectral slope)는 기류 그래프의 기울기(time derivatives; 도함수)의 불연속성의 정도와 관련이 있습니다.
Due to the waveform skewing discussed earlier, the most dominant source of discontinuity often occurs around the instant of main excitation when the time derivative of the glottal flow waveform returns from the negative peak to zero within a time scale of Ta (Fig. (Fig.4).4).
기류가 성문 면적 변화를 약간 늦게 따라가는 특성 때문에 이러한 불연속성은 주로 기울기가 음수에서 제로로 변하는 순간 일어납니다.
For an abrupt glottal flow cutoff (Ta = 0), the time derivative of the glottal flow waveform has a strong discontinuity at the point of main excitation, which causes the voice source spectrum to decay asymptotically at a roll-off rate of −6 dB per octave toward high frequencies.
이러한 불연속성 때문에 한 옥타브 올라갈때마다 스펙트럼은 6dB씩 감소합니다.
Increasing Ta from zero leads to a gradual return from the negative peak to zero.
위 그래프에서 Ta를 늘릴 수록 기류의 도함수는 더 완만하게 0으로 돌아옵니다.
When approximated by an exponential function, this gradual return functions as a lower-pass filter, with a cutoff frequency around 1/Ta, and reduces the excitation of harmonics above the cutoff frequency 1/Ta.
그렇게 되면 1/Ta 아래의 저주파 대역은 증폭되고 1/Ta 위의 고주파 대역은 깎여나갑니다.
Thus, in the frequency range concerning voice perception, increasing Ta often leads to reduced higher-order harmonic excitation.
그래서 Ta를 올리면 고주파 배음들이 감쇄됩니다.
In the extreme case when there is minimal vocal fold contact, the time derivative of the glottal flow waveform is so smooth that the voice source spectrum only has a few lower-order harmonics.
그래서 성대의 접촉이 거의 없는 기식 발성에서는 고주파 배음들은 거의 사라지고 저주파 배음들만 남게 되는 것입니다.
Perceptually, strong excitation of higher-order harmonics is often associated with a bright output sound quality, whereas voice source with limited excitation of higher-order harmonics is often perceived to be weak.
고주파 배음들이 강하게 살아있으면 밝은 소리로 들리고 반면에 고주파 배음들이 죽으면 약한 소리로 느껴집니다.
Also of perceptual importance is the turbulence noise produced immediately downstream of the glottis.
성문 바로 위쪽에서 발생하는 와류도 음질에 중요하게 작용합니다.
Although small in amplitude, the noise component plays an important role in voice quality perception, particularly for female voice in which aspiration noise is more persistent than in male voice.
이러한 와류음은 소리는 작지만 음질에 큰 영향을 끼칩니다. 특히 기식 발성이 많은 여성에서 그렇습니다.
While the noise component of voice is often modeled as white noise, its spectrum often is not flat and may exhibit different spectral shapes, depending on the glottal opening and flow rate as well as the vocal tract shape.
실제로 성대에서 발생하는 노이즈는 화이트 노이즈가 아니라 약간의 스펙트럼 모양을 가지고 있습니다. 이 모양은 성대가 열린 정도와 기류의 속도에 따라 결정됩니다.
Interaction between the spectral shape and relative levels of harmonic and noise energy in the voice source has been shown to influence the perception of voice quality (Kreiman and Gerratt, 2012).
이러한 노이즈의 스펙트럼 모양, 그리고 배음에 비해 노이즈의 강도가 얼마나 강한지가 음질에 영향이 있습니다.
It is worth noting that many of the source parameters are not independent from each other and often co-vary.
성대 원음의 여러 패러미터들은 서로 영향을 끼칩니다.
How they co-vary at different voicing conditions, which is essential to natural speech synthesis, remains to be the focus of many studies (e.g., Sundberg and Hogset, 2001; Gobl and Chasaide, 2003; Patel et al., 2011).
그건 아직 연구가 많이 없으니 좀 더 연구 해야 합니다.
